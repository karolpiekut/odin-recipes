Chicken and beef broth

Extremely rich, healthy and full of minerals. A remedy to cold used for generations - it's truly a liquid gold!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds beef bones
  • 1 chicken carcass plus wings
  • 6 cups cool water, or as needed
  • 2 tablespoons cider vinegar
  • 2 onions
  • 2 carrots
  • 1 parsnip
  • allspice
  • 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Fresh sage
  • Fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Pour water into large pot, start boiling it
  2. Add bones to the pot along with all spices and cider vinegar
  3. Wash veg, no need to peel them.
  4. Slice veg in half
  5. Add veg and herbs to the pot
  6. Once it starts to boil again, set the heat to low
  7. Cook under the lid for minimum four hours, but longer if possible to make the best out of it
  8. Once fully cooked, pour it through the sieve into the jars
  9. You can store it in the fridge once it cools down
